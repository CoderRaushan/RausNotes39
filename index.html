Frequently Asked Questions

Let's Talk Solutions

Q1.What types of notes are available on your website?

Our website offers comprehensive notes for B.Tech, including IT and CSE, as well as Physics and Chemistry groups. You can find detailed study materials to support your academic journey in these subjects.

Q2.Are the notes free to access?

Yes, all the notes on our website are freely accessible. We believe in making education and study resources easily available to everyone.

Q3.How can I use the SGPA calculator on your website?

Utilizing the SGPA calculator is simple. Enter your marks into the designated fields, and the calculator will provide you with your Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA). It's a handy tool for students to estimate their academic performance.

Q4.Are the SGPA calculations based on a specific grading system?

Yes, the SGPA calculator on our website follows the standard grading system used in educational institutions. It takes into account your marks and converts them into the corresponding grade points, eventually calculating the SGPA.

Q5.Are there any specific requirements to access the notes and calculator?

No special requirements are needed. You can freely access the notes and use the calculator without creating an account. We aim to make the learning experience as seamless as possible.

Q6.Can I request specific topics or subjects for notes to be added to the website?

Absolutely! We encourage users to suggest topics or subjects they would like to see covered in our notes. You can contact us through our feedback form or email, and we will do our best to accommodate your requests in future updates.
